To Boost or not to Boost

If you opened this page with the boosted link, the styles should be consistent with with the rest of the site, because the boosting means that any stylesheets and scripts that are linked to the index page are also linked to this page, and any that are loaded in the head of this HTML are ignored.

If you opened this page with the un-boosted link, the styles and scripts that are loaded in the head of this HTML are applied, which is why you had to contend with that alert and why your eyes are bleeding from the terrible styles.
